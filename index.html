Mareva Millarc

Bio

Mareva Millarc was born in Hollywood, California in 1954.

After moving within the US and abroad, she now resides in Middletown Springs, Vermont where she shares a home with her husband Peter.

Motivated by the love of painting Millarc creates art that is free of representational reference, delving into each piece with intuition and vision.

Painting with oil, acrylic, ink, or mixed media, her avid use of bold line-work, organic shapes, and vibrant colors highlight the multiple layer designs often found in her work.

With no reference to the real world, Millarc eagerly explores the wild places where hidden feelings dwell, sharing her experiences as something to behold.
